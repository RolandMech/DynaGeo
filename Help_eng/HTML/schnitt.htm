Intersection

Intersection of two lines    

(automatically repeatable)

Click with the left mouse button on the two lines, line segments or circles in question. The point of intersection is constructed and displayed.

If there are two intersection points, e.g. when intersecting a circle with a straight line, both will be created and displayed. If there are up to four intersection points, e.g. when intersecting a hyperbola with a circle, then all valid intersection points are created and displayed. If, however, DynaGeo notes that one of them already exists, then only the new points will be constructed.
