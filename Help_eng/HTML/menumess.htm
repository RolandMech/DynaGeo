Measure Menu

This menu includes commands to measure distances and angle widths and to use numbers and expressions in your drawing. Additionally, it includes instructions affecting the co-ordinate system and manipulating points with fixed co-ordinates.

Most of the commands of this menu can be activated from the menu bar Measure&Calculate .

    Measure angle width
    Measure area
