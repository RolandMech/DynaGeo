Triangle    

(automatically repeatable)

Click with the left mouse button at the positions of the 3 vertices to draw the triangle.

If you click in the working area where there is no point, a new point is created at this position.

You also have the option of re-using an existing line segment as one side of a triangle. When you click on a line segment, its end points are used as two vertices of a new triangle.
