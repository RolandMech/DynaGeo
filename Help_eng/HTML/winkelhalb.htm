Bisector    

(automatically repeatable)

Click on the three points defining the angle you wish to bisect. Keep to the following order:

  1. Click on a point at the first side of the angle.
  2. Click on the vertex.
  3. Click on a point at the other side of the angle.

If the angle is already marked with an angle arc (see the command Mark angle), you can just click on this arc, too.

The bisector is created and displayed.



ALT variant: Exterior bisector

    If you start this command by clicking the tool button on the Construct bar, and you hold down the ALT key while you click, then the same parameter objects are expected, but instead of the bisector of the indicated angle, the bisector of the exterior angle is created.
