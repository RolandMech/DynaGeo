Ellipse from centre and 2 conjugate points    

This command creates an ellipse with a given centre point, that runs through two given conjugate points.

First click the point that is to serve as centre, then click the two conjugate points. Finally, the appropriate ellipse is created and displayed.

    To construct the vertices and the axes of this ellipse, DynaGeo internally calculates the famous "Rytz construction" that is well known to all friends of the descriptive geometry!
