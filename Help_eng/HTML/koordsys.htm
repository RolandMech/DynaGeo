Co-ordinate system    

This command opens a dialogue box allowing you to select how to display the co-ordinate system:

  • On the "Shape" panel you decide about the outer appearance of the co-ordinate system:
    • First you may choose if the origin of the co-ordinate system will be visible or not. If you deselect this option, the co-ordinate system will be totally hidden.

    • Second you may choose if the axis of the co-ordinate system will be visible or not. If you deselect this option, the next two options are automatically disabled.
    • Third you may choose if the axis will be lettered with numbers or not.
    • Forth you may choose if a grid will be displayed or not. If you select this option, you can specify the distance of the marks ("narrow" or "medium" or "wide") and the type of the grid marks (a small "cross" at every grid point or "solid line" or "dotted line").
  • On the "Names of the axis" panel you decide about the names written to the axis. The normal values are "x" and "y".

  • On the "Scaling" panel you decide about the unity width on both axis:
    Normally, you should use the same unit length on both axis. You may choose the length of the unit in pixels. For special situations, you may even specify different units for the two axis, but beware of the consequences of this decision!
If you close this window by clicking the "Okay" button, the co-ordinate system will be refreshed in the new style. A click on the "Cancel" button will discard the changes and leave the co-ordinate system unchanged.
