Mark an angle

Mark angle

(automatically repeatable)

Click with the (left) mouse on three points that define the angle. Keep to the following order:

  1. a point on the first side of the angle
  2. the angle's vertex
  3. a point on the other side of the angle.

Then an arc is drawn to mark the angle.

Please note, that this arc is not a true EUKLID DynaGeo object of your drawing but a decorative element like names, distance lines or text boxes. Especially, you cannot re-use such an angle arc as a parent for other objects.
