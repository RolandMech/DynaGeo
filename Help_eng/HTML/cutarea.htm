Cut a filling   

(automatically repeatable)

This command allows to get even more complicated areas filled: you can cut any filling along an line of intersection. Possible lines of intersection are lines, polygons and circles.

First click on the filling you want to cut, then indicate the intersection line, and at last click on that part of the filling that you want to keep. Only this part of the area will appear filled, the other one is cut away.
