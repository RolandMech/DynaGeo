Further details about angles

You can change the radius of the angle arc by dragging it.

If the angle is a right one, it is marked in a special way: instead of the normal angle arc a right angled hook is displayed. If you don't like this you can choose right angles to be marked with an arc and an additional point: go to Options | Presentation and check the box "Mark right angles with a point"

On the page Options | Measurements, you can choose if EUKLID DynaGeo is to take care of the orientation of angles. If you check the option "Care for angle orientation" then all angles are assumed to have mathematically positiv orientation, i.e. they are oriented counter-clockwise. If you uncheck this option, angles of more than 180° are avoided: if necessary, the orientation is switched.

On the tabsheet Options | Internals you can set the width of the interval around 90° inside which the angle is assumed to be a right one.


Please note:

    Angle arcs are not regular objects for EUKLID DynaGeo, i.e. they cannot serve as parents for other objects. Like names, measurements and comment texts, they are only decorative elements.
