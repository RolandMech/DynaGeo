Insert a segment

This command extends the capabilities of DynaGeo beyond the reach of compass and ruler by providing an "insertion ruler". This is one of the classical tools for solving problems that cannot be overcome with circle and ruler alone, as e.g. the division of any angle in 3 equal parts.

When you call this command, a small window appears where you can enter the length s (in cm) of the segment to insert. Then with the mouse click a point P and finally two straight lines g1 and g2.

Then DynaGeo will construct a new straight line h running through P in such a direction that g1 and g2 cut a segment of length s out of h.


Attention !
    This problem can have up to 4 different solutions. So don't be surprised that the construction may "jump" while you drag it: when the actually shown solution becomes invalid, DynaGeo will just switch to another one (if there is any).
