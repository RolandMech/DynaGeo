Unsnap textbox from object

Click with the left mouse button on the bound textbox you wish to release. To show that the binding was terminated successfully, the textbox will move away a bit from its former reference object.

Then the textbox will no more follow any object's moves, but always keep its position relativ to the left upper corner of the drawing window.
