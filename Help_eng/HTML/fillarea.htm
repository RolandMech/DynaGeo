Fill area   

(automatically repeatable)

This command serves to fill a polygon or a circle. When you click on the border line of the desired area, it will be filled using the actual fill colour and pattern.

To the right of the command button there are two attribute symbols showing the actual values of the fill colour and the fill pattern. If you click on a filled area of your drawing it will adopt these attribute values. If on the other hand you want to change an attribute's value, just click the appropriate button: a local menu appears where you can choose the new value. The following table shows the attribute values available:

    Attribute Possible values
    Fill colour A click will open the standard colour dialogue of WINDOWS where you can choose the desired colour. After closing the dialogue with Okay the "Colour" symbol will take on the new colour, e.g.:
    (if you chose "red")
    Fill pattern



You can even fill a half plane: just take a line as border of the area you want to fill. In this case you must indicate additionally which of the two possible half planes is to be filled.
