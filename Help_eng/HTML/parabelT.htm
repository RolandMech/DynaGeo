Parabola from 2 curve points and their tangent directions    

This command creates a parabola running through 2 given points with 2 given tangent directions in these points.

Click on the first point the parabola is to run through, then click a straight line that is parallel to the desired direction. Next click on the second point, then click a second straight line. Finally, the parabola is created and displayed.

    The two straight lines may or may not run through the respective curve point. Internally, only the direction of the lines is used, not their absolute position.
