Options | Text settings

Standard font for labels

    This section allows you to choose a standard font for all labels in your drawing. You can choose the font name and the font size, but no attributes (like "fat") are allowed. The preview window is automatically updated when you change the font. Although you can choose any of the installed fonts of your WINDOWS system, it is strongly recommended not to choose any exotic settings. In most cases it seems sufficient to set the font size according to your needs and leave the rest unchanged.

No names and measures in construction texts

    If you check this box the construction text will only show the true geometrical objects of your drawing, but no name objects or measurement objects. Leaving this box unchecked will lead to a complete list of all objects created.

Form of the line equations

    Here you can choose if DynaGeo should show the line equation either in the form "y = m x + c" or in the general form "ax + by = c".
