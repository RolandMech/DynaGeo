Snap point to line    

(automatically repeatable)

First click the point you want to snap to a line, then click the line. The point will now move onto the line, dragging with it all its depending objects. The following objects can serve as carrier lines:

  • straight lines (i.e. lines, segments, rays)
  • circles and arcs
  • polygons
  • locus lines
  • conics

When later you drag this point it will move only on its carrier line. It cannot leave this line until you released the snapping.


Please note:

    Only basis points can be snapped to carrier lines. However, a point can never be snapped to a line that is constructed with reference to this point and so is depending on it.
