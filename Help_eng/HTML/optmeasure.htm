Care for angle orientation:

    This switch defines if EUKLID DynaGeo should pay attention to the orientation (<180 or >180) of any angles input.
    See also Further details about angles.
    Normally checked.

Measure polygon and circle areas with orientation:

    If you check this box not only integral areas will be measured oriented but also the elementary areas of polygons and circles.
    Normally unchecked.

Maximal number of intervals in a Riemann sum:

    Here you can decide the maximum of how many slim rectangles may contribute to a Riemann sum. Though no upper limit for the range is specified, please keep in mind that the calculation of a Riemann sum may be rather time-consuming for greater numbers of summands.
    Range : [ 1 .... (256) .... (?) ]
