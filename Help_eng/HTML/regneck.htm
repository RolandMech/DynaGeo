Regular polygon

After you selected this command, a window appears where you can enter the number of vertices your regular polygon should have. After closing the window, click on two points that are to become neighboring vertices of the polygon. You can use existing points or create new ones by clicking at an empty spot of the drawing.

Finally, the regular polygon is created and displayed. Its orientation is choosen in the way that its centre lies on the left side when you move from the first clicked vertex to the second one.

When in the "Options" you checked the box "Fill new polygons on creation" on the register page "Presentation", then the new polygon appears filled.
