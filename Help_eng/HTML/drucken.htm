Print    

Prints the current drawing.

Unfortunately, not all black & white printers reproduce screen colours properly. This is due to inappropriate printer drivers and/or limitations in the WINDOWS system itself. Be careful with the choice of colours in your drawings if this is the case for your printer.

One more problem with many printers are the WINDOWS line styles. Some printers cannot print dashed or dotted lines correctly. To avoid these difficulties, you can make EUKLID DynaGeo simulate these attributes during printing (see Options | Export).
