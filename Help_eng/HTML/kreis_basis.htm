Basis circle    

(automatically repeatable)

Click with the left mouse button at any position in the working area and keep the button pressed while you move the mouse. A circle is drawn. Its centre is the position where you first clicked the mouse and it runs through the actual mouse position. When the mouse button is released this basis circle is complete.

In contrast to a normal circle you cannot change the radius of a basis circle afterwards. If you drag a basis circle, you move its position. Its radius is not changed, and its centre is not displayed.
