Print preview

Shows a preview of the printed page.


Attention !

    The Preview only allows to foresee which part of the drawing will be shown on the printed page. The style of the objects may vary between the preview and the printed output. This is due to the limited capabilities of some printers. Especially if you try to print a coloured drawing on a monochrome printer, it may happen that some objects in light colours seem to be missing in the printout.
