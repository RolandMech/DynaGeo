Snap textbox to object

Click with the left mouse button on the textbox, then on the object the textbox is to be bound to. The textbox will now move near the object. Suitable objects to bind textbox to are all points and lines of your drawing. If the textbox was bound to another object before, the old binding is released before the new one is established.

During all following dragging actions the textbox will follow all movements of its reference object. However, you can adjust its position relativ to the reference object by dragging the textbox.
