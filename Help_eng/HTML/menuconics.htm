Conics Menu

The conics menu contains cammands to create ellipses, parabolas and hyperbolas (including the degenerate cases). Beneath construction commands that rely on the focus properties of the conics there are some more commands to construct these conics from other properties, too. All of the commands in this menu are available via the Curve bar.


    Ellipse from 2 focal points and 1 curve point
    Ellipse from centre, main vertex and a m.a.c. point
    Ellipse from centre and 2 conjugate points

    Parabola from focal point and directrix
    Parabola from 2 curve points and their tangent directions

    Hyperbola from 2 focal points and 1 curve point
    Hyperbola from 2 asymptotes und 1 curve point

    General conic through 5 points

    Polar point from conic and polar line
