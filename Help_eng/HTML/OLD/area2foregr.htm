Move area to foreground    

With this command you can control the mutual overlapping of areas:

when you click on an area that is partially covered by another one, then the clicked area is put into the foreground; so it becomes totally visible and the other polygon is covered to some extent.

It is even possible to bring a totally covered area to the foreground if only you know its name: every area automatically gets a name, that starts with "F_" and continues with a number. If you choose the Bring area to foreground command and click at a spot where several areas overlap, a listbox comes up to let you choose the desired one.
