Hyperbola from 2 focal points and 1 curve point    

This command creates a hyperbola with two given focal points, that runs through a given third point.

First click on the two points that are to serve as focal points, and then click on the third point the hyperbola is to run through. Finally, the appropriate hyperbola is created and displayed.
