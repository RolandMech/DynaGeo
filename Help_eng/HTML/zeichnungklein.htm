Reduce drawing

Reduces the drawing in the working area.

The centre of reduction is the centre of the actual drawing window. Initially, the zoom factor is 1.5, but you can set it to any desired value (menu item Miscellaneous | Options, tabsheet Internals).

This command zooms the co-ordinate system and all constructed objects of your drawing. Only number objects and (unbound) textboxes will not be moved: they are always placed relative to the left upper corner of the drawing window.
