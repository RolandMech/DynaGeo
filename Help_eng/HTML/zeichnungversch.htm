Move drawing

Moves the entire drawing.

Selecting this command turns the mouse arrow into a hand. Then you can move the drawing by pressing the left mouse button and keeping it down while moving the mouse. Releasing the button leaves the drawing at the current position, and the mouse cursor changes back to its normal shape.

It's faster to use a short-cut:

    Point with the mouse to an empty area on the drawing, hold down the Shift key while pressing the left mouse button, and then move the mouse.
